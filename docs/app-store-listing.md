## Description
Track every item across all your Diablo 2 Resurrected mules — then find anything instantly.

D2 Mule Tracker is an offline inventory catalog for Diablo 2 Resurrected. Catalog items on your mule characters and shared stashes, then search across all of them to find any item in seconds. No more logging into every mule to find your Shako.

FEATURES:
• Instant autocomplete search across 1,300+ items — uniques, sets, runewords, bases, runes, and gems
• Cross-mule search: type "shako" and see exactly which mule has it, with roll notes
• Variable stat ranges shown when adding items — know what to note for a quality trade listing
• Socket tracking on base items — mark your 4os Monarch or 5os Phase Blade
• Supports all eras: Classic, Lord of Destruction, and Reign of the Warlock
• Organize by realm: Softcore/Hardcore, Ladder/Non-Ladder, Americas/Europe/Asia
• Bulk select, move, and delete items between mules
• Swipe-to-delete and swipe-to-archive for fast management
• Sort and filter by category, name, or date added
• Export/import JSON backups via share sheet (save to iCloud Drive, send to another device)
• Dark gothic theme with Diablo 2 item rarity colors
• 100% offline — no internet required, no account needed, no data collected

ITEM DATABASE:
Complete item database sourced from blizzhackers/d2data, covering all D2R 3.0 content including Reign of the Warlock items (grimoires, Colossal Ancient jewels, new uniques, sets, and runewords). Common nicknames are searchable — try "shako", "hoto", "botd", "cta", "soj", or "grief".

PRIVACY:
Your data stays on your device. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts, no servers. Just you and your mules.
